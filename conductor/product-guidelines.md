# Product Guidelines

## Prose Style
The content on this blog should adopt a prose style that is both **Formal and Objective**, emphasizing factual reporting, academic tone, and minimal bias, while also being **Engaging and Conversational**, using a more personal voice and storytelling to connect with the reader.

## Brand Messaging
The core message and voice of the brand should be multifaceted:
- **Authoritative and Trustworthy:** The brand must be perceived as a credible and reliable source of information.
- **Inspirational and Uplifting:** The content should aim to inspire and motivate the audience, providing a positive outlook.
- **Provocative and Challenging:** The brand should not shy away from controversial topics and should be willing to challenge conventional wisdom, encouraging critical thought.

## Visual Identity
The overall visual identity or aesthetic of the blog should be **Classic and Traditional**, evoking a sense of timelessness and using more conventional layouts and design elements.
