# Product Definition

## Initial Concept
A blog application focusing on current affairs from a religious and political perspective.

## Purpose
The primary purpose of this application is content management, specifically for a blog.

## Target Audience
The primary target audience for this application consists of individuals who actively follow current affairs, with a particular interest in religious and political perspectives.

## Key Features
- **User Authentication and Authorization:** Allowing users to sign up, log in, and potentially manage different roles or permissions within the platform.
- **Content Creation and Editing:** Providing tools for authors to create, edit, and publish blog posts, including a rich text editor and draft management.
- **Commenting and Discussion Features:** Enabling readers to engage with content through comments, fostering community and discussion.
- **Search and Filtering Capabilities:** Allowing users to easily find relevant articles through search functionality and content filtering.
- **Newsletter Subscription:** Providing a mechanism for users to subscribe to a newsletter to receive updates and new content.

## Monetization and Conversion Goals
The application aims to monetize and convert users through a combination of:
- **Ad Revenue:** Displaying advertisements to generate income.
- **Subscription-based Premium Content:** Offering exclusive content or features through a subscription model.
- **Product Sales:** Selling physical or digital products related to the blog's niche.
